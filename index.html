Character Generator

I was familiar with name generators, but i wanted to have my own spin on it by adding a few extra features.

  • Names composed of different sylabols
  • A few demographic details about the character
  • A stat list for each character

The characters come out pretty silly, but I thought it was fun.

Percentage Clock

On hard days its nice to know how much of the day you need to get through before its over. Percentage clock gives it in a simple number. It doesn't provide much comfort as i got a little carried away with the css animation library. A few features are:

  • Kind of tells you the time
  • Transitions from red to green throughout the day
  • Pulses like your heart does
  • You can press on the number and it makes an annoying noise that raises in pitch and makes you feel uneasy

Wouldn't recommend this one

Manticore Generator

A friend of mine saw the character generator and was working on making some manticores for a personal project. they asked if i could make something similar to help them with the process. I added a few extra features to help them:

  • Random chance of not adding tail/wings
  • Able to lock body parts so they aren't randomised

Otherwise it is very similar to CharacterGenerator
