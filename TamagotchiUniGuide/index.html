How To Use:

  • Select what Tama you have - else select "Other"
    • If your Tama is not an adult they wont be listed
    • If your Tama is from a DLC I was too lazy to list it
  • Select the personality group of your Tama
    • Most likes/dislikes are determined by your personality group
    • Selecting "None" will only show the likes/dislikes determined by your Tama
Guide To Determing Personality Group

There are 5 personality groups, each including 6 personalities.

Group Personalities
Docile Calm, Cool, Honest, Orderly, Polite
Energetic Bold, Careless, Jolly, Naughty, Passionate, Playful
Shy Coy, Delicate, Fearful, Reserved, Shy, Timid
Spoiled Affectionate, Emotional, Friendly, Lonely, Lovely, Spoiled
Mysterious Chill, Ditzy, Moody, Mysterious, Rash, Relaxed

For more information, see Tamagotchi Wiki
